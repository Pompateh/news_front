Client Process

Design Process

01.

INITIAL CONSULTATION & DISCOVERY

We start with a deep dive into your business, goals, and vision. This step involves understanding your project scope, target audience, and desired outcomes. We'll discuss your preferences, analyze your competition, and define the project scope.

02.

TAILORED PROPOSAL

Based on the insights from our consultation, I'll create a customized proposal. This will typically include multiple design concepts, a detailed project timeline, list of deliverables, and pricing for each option.

03.

AGREEMENT & ONBOARDING

Once you've selected your package, we'll sign the contract, complete any necessary questionnaires, and set timeline for 50% of the total project fee. I'll also provide guidance on collecting visual inspiration for your project. Once the contract is signed and the deposit are received, the project is officially booked.

04.

FINALIZATION & HANDOVER

After all deliverables are approved, I'll send the final invoice for the remaining project fee. Upon payment, I'll transfer all assets in a well-organized, user-friendly folder. Your brand is now ready to launch and make a lasting impact!
